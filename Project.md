# **LOREN_AI  Early Diabetes Risk Analysis System**

## 1. Project Overview
This project analyzes healthcare and lifestyle indicators to identify diabetes risk patterns using patient medical data. The system studies important health factors such as BMI, blood glucose level, HbA1c level, hypertension, heart disease, age, and smoking history to support early diabetes awareness and preventive healthcare.

---

## 2. Problem Statement
Diabetes is one of the fastest-growing chronic diseases worldwide. Many individuals ignore early warning signs because they do not understand medical indicators or healthcare risks. Delayed detection often leads to severe complications such as heart disease, kidney problems, and poor metabolic health.

This project aims to analyze healthcare indicators and identify important factors contributing to diabetes risk using data analytics and machine learning.

---

## 3. Project Objective

### Primary Objective
To analyze healthcare data and identify important diabetes risk indicators using machine learning and healthcare analytics.

### Secondary Objectives
- Understand relationships between medical indicators and diabetes
- Analyze BMI, HbA1c level, and blood glucose impact
- Identify high-risk patient patterns
- Build predictive models for diabetes risk detection
- Provide healthcare insights for preventive care

---

## 4. Dataset Description

The dataset contains healthcare and lifestyle-related information of patients used for diabetes risk analysis.

### Dataset Features

| Feature | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| gender | Gender of patient |
| age | Age of patient |
| hypertension | Presence of hypertension (0/1) |
| heart_disease | Presence of heart disease (0/1) |
| smoking_history | Smoking behavior of patient |
| bmi | Body Mass Index |
| HbA1c_level | Average blood sugar level |
| blood_glucose_level | Blood glucose measurement |
| diabetes | Diabetes status (Target Variable) |

### Target Variable
**diabetes**
- 0 → Non-Diabetic
- 1 → Diabetic
